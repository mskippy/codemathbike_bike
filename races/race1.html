Race 1 – Richard Juryn XC

Opening race of the NSSSAA Mountain Bike League season at Richard Juryn Trail. Riders will complete multiple laps of the upper course before finishing through the lower section.

Race Details

  • Date: Wednesday, April 8th
  • Location: Richard Juryn Trail
  • Registration: 3:00 - 3:30 PM
  • Race Start: 3:30 PM

Trailforks Route

Open route in Trailforks

Course Description

Riders start near the trail entrance and climb into the upper loop before completing multiple laps depending on their division. The course features flowing singletrack, short punchy climbs, and fast descents before returning to the finish area.

Race Order

Start Time Division Laps
3:00 PM Juvenile Boys / Juvenile Girls 2
3:15 PM Bantam Boys / Bantam Girls 2
4:00 PM Senior Boys / Senior Girls 3
4:10 PM Junior Boys / Junior Girls 3

Additional Notes

  • Please arrive early to allow time for registration.
  • Riders should warm up off the race course.
  • Follow marshal instructions and respect other trail users.
  • Helmets must be worn at all times while riding.

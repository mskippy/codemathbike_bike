Race #4 – Fromme Enduro

Enduro-style race at Mount Fromme featuring multiple timed downhill stages.

  • Date: Wednesday, April 29
  • Location: Mount Fromme
  • Lead School: Argyle
  • Registration: Water Towers / upper Fromme parking area
  • Parking:
    • Upper Fromme parking lot
    • Coleman Street area
    • Please carpool or arrange drop-off where possible

Race Day Schedule

Time Details
2:00 PM Registration / chip pickup opens
2:45 PM Rider briefing
3:00 PM Riders released in waves
  • 1st Wave - 10s/11s/12s
  • 2nd Wave - 8s/9s
3:30 PM Timing starts

Riders should arrive early, check in, and be ready with all required gear.

Course

Riders will climb to each stage and complete timed downhill sections. Total combined stage times determine overall results. Transfer stages are not timed.

Grade 8/9 (Bant/Juv)

Open Grade 8/9 route in Trailforks

Grade 10/11/12 (Jr/Sr)

Open Grade 10/11/12 route in Trailforks

Race Notes

  • Riders are responsible for knowing their course.
  • Stay on designated race and transfer routes.
  • Be respectful of all trail users.
  • Ride in control and within your ability.
  • Helmets required at ALL times. No headphones.
  • All timing chips must be returned.

Marshal Information

Marshal Map

Sign Up to Marshal

Marshals help direct riders, manage trail access, and support safe race flow.

Starting Marshals

  • Start riders at the assigned intervals
  • Manage rider flow and staging area
  • Collect timing equipment and signage
  • Sweep course after final rider

Finish Marshals

  • Manage riders at stage finish
  • Direct riders to the next transfer or finish area
  • Collect timing equipment

Course Marshals

  • Direct riders at intersections
  • Manage trail crossings
  • Assist public trail users

What to Bring

  • Bike - useful for accessing marshal locations
  • Backpack - to carry signage or timing equipment
  • Phone - your number will be used for race day communication

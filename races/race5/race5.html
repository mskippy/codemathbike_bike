Race #5 – Squamish Enduro

Squamish-based enduro race featuring multiple technical stages and longer transfers between stages.

  • Date: Wednesday, May 6
  • Location: Alice Lake Provincial Park, Squamish
  • Lead School: CMA + Howe Sound
  • Registration: Alice Lake Picnic Area - by the docks
  • Parking: Day Parking Lot
    • Limited parking available
    • Carpool strongly recommended
    • Follow signage and parking directions on arrival

Race Day Schedule

Time Details
2:00 PM Registration / chip pickup opens
2:45 PM Rider briefing (mandatory)
3:00 PM Riders released in waves
  • 1st Wave - Grade 10/11/12
  • 2nd Wave - Grade 8/9
3:30 PM Timing begins

Riders must arrive early, check in, and be prepared to start on time.

Course

Riders will complete multiple timed downhill stages connected by transfer climbs. Only stage times count toward results. Transfers are not timed but must be completed within the race window.

Grade 8/9 (Bant/Juv)

  • Stage 1: Leave of Absence
  • Stage 2: Pamplemousse → Lower Pamplemousse → Credit Line
  • Note: Spicy features will be taped off

Open Grade 8/9 route in Trailforks

Grade 10/11/12 (Jr/Sr)

  • Stage 1: Rupert
  • Stage 2: Pamplemousse → Lower Pamplemousse → Credit Line
  • Stage 3: Crouching Squirrel Hidden Monkey → Kiki → Rico
  • Note: Spicy features will be taped off

Open Grade 10/11/12 route in Trailforks

Race Notes

  • Riders are responsible for knowing their course and stage order.
  • There will be no course marshals directing riders between stages.
  • Stay on designated race and transfer routes at all times.
  • Respect all trail users — these are active public trails.
  • Ride in control and within your ability on all stages.
  • Helmets are required at ALL times. No headphones.
  • Plan your time and effort — transfers are not timed but must be completed.
  • Failure to follow course markings may result in disqualification.
  • All timing chips must be returned immediately after the race.

Race #6 – Whistler XC League Finals

The final race of the 2026 NSSSAA Mountain Bike League season takes place in Whistler at Cheakamus.

  • Date: Wednesday, May 13, 2026
  • Location: Interpretive Forest Parking Lots, Cheakamus
  • Lead School: Whistler Secondary
  • Registration: Interpretive Forest Parking Lot
  • Parking & Directions:
    • Turn right onto Cheakamus Lake Road off Highway 99 at Function Junction if coming from Vancouver or Squamish. It is the first left into the parking lot.
    • If coming from Pemberton, turn left onto Cheakamus Lake Road.
    • Additional parking is available further along Cheakamus Lake Road (angle parking).
    • House Rock overflow parking: follow Cheakamus Lake Road for about 500 m, turn onto Cheakamus Lake FSR (before the bridge), then take the right onto the next FSR, cross the bridge, and take the first left.

Race Notes

  • Please check in at registration when you arrive.
  • Riders should be ready to race at their scheduled start time.
  • Both courses start on Cheakamus Lake Road.
  • Make sure you know which course applies to your grade category before race time.
  • This is the league final, so be prepared to stay for team and individual awards after racing.
